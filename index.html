Who We Are

The House Behind the Work

My name is Michael. I'm 30, I'm from Hartford, and I built Dominion House because I wanted to be the resource I wish I had growing up.

When you work with me, you're not a client — you're family. Because when you win, you're literally helping feed mine. That's not a slogan. That's real.

Dominion House is built on one word: ownership. Own your brand. Own your mistakes. Own your future. The Blue Heron is our symbol because it's everything we aim to be — patient, precise, confident, and unbothered by what's around it.

I'm not building this to be famous. I'm building this to be free — and to bring as many people with me as I can. From the kid in the hood who thought school wasn't for him, to the entrepreneur who's ready to go bigger. If you're trying to be better, there's a room in this house for you.

The vision goes further than web services and clothing. One day Dominion House gives back — free clothes from the brand, marketing help for people learning new skills, resources for people who've hit rock bottom. Because I've been there too.

This is just the beginning.
— Michael, Founder
